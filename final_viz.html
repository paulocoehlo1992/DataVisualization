This visualization depicts the survival rate (%) of passengers travelling across different passenger classof Titanic .There was three passenger classes in Titanic namely class 1, class 2, and class 3 . We can clearly see that survival rate was highest for class 1 across both gender categories,male and female .It can also be observed that female passengershad comparatively higher survival rate than male passengers across all classes. This can be attributed to the fact that there were some bias towards female passengers and class 1 passengers in the rescue mission after the crisis.
